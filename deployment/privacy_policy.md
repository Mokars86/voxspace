# Privacy Policy for VoxSpace

**Last updated: January 09, 2026**

VoxSpace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by VoxSpace.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect information just to provide our services:
*   **Account Information:** Username, email address.
*   **User Content:** Messages, posts, and files stored in "My Bag".
*   **Device Information:** Device type, OS version (for diagnostics).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
*   To provide and maintain our Service.
*   To notify you about changes to our Service.
*   To allow you to participate in interactive features (Chat, Calls, Space).
*   To provide customer support.

## 3. Data Storage
*   **My Bag:** Files stored in "My Bag" are private. We enforce a 50MB limit for free users.
*   **Security:** We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@voxspace.app
